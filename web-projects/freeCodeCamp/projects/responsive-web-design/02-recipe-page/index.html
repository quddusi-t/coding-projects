Egg fried rice

Welcome to our recipe page! Today we're sharing a delicious and nutritious twist on classic fried rice - Egg Fried Rice with Leek and Broccoli. This colorful dish combines the mild, sweet flavor of leeks with vibrant broccoli florets, all brought together with fluffy scrambled eggs and perfectly seasoned rice. It's a wonderful way to transform leftover rice into a satisfying meal that's both comforting and packed with vegetables. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old and cooled)
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • 1 medium leek, white and light green parts only, sliced thin
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3-4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add broccoli florets and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until bright green and tender-crisp, then remove and set aside
  3. Add another tablespoon of olive oil to the pan
  4. Add sliced leeks and cook for 2-3 minutes until softened
  5. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant
  6. Push vegetables to one side of the pan and add remaining olive oil
  7. Beat eggs and scramble them in the empty space of the pan
  8. Add the cooked rice, breaking up any clumps with your spatula
  9. Return the broccoli to the pan and mix everything together
  10. Add soy sauce and stir to coat evenly
  11. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  12. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, stirring frequently, until heated through
  13. Serve immediately, garnished with green onions if desired
